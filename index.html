Our Focus

We aim to teach students new skills and allow them to apply the skills they learned in class/ in the club to hands on exercises.

Interactive Workshops

Our organization holds weekly workshops during the semester to teach members new things in the security industry.

Community

We've built a thriving discord community that allows members to discuss current security events, share resources, and network.

Competition

We attend multiple competitions throughout the year focusing on red/blue/purple teaming & we even host our own competition, GDDC!
